About me

I'm a PhD cognitive scientist turned to data science. In my PhD, I used machine learning and text mining to show that what people write on social media predicts a lot about them as a person. For example, I showed that people's tweets can predict their decisions and people's Reddit posts may predict whether they have a mental illness. Now, I want to apply my background to applied problems in data science. I'm starting an Insight data science fellowship in Janaury 2020!
